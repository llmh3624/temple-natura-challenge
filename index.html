About Us

Our Philosophy

Temple Natura is a serene urban oasis in Palma that has been offering a unique dining experience for the past 15 years. They promote a healthy lifestyle by creating artisanal vegan and vegetarian cuisine infused with cultural flavors. Their menu highlights local and seasonal ingredients from Mediterranean cuisine and the aromatic spices of Colombian dishes. The tranquil space is crowned by a century-old mandarin tree, providing a unique dining experience where visitors can indulge in delectable food and drink while being surrounded by nature.

Join us at Temple Natura and treat yourself to a one-of-a-kind dining experience that nourishes both your body and soul.
